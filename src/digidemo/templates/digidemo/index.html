Political Info for Canadians By Canadians

Crowdsourced by Canadians like you

Our issues are crowdsourced by Canadians like you, and written through a community authoring process, to promote a balanced account that reflects diverse perspectives.

A non-partisan source you can trust

The goal of this platform is to create a productive dialogue that focused on learning and finding solutions. We want to cut through rhetoric, while still reflecting the diversity of viewpoints, and provide a place where Canadians can both read about current events and take action to shape them.

It's easy, with a little help from your friends

Is there an important issue going on today that you think Canadians need to know about? It only takes five minutes to start a new issue, and invite others to help you. Our tools make it easy to share the work of staying active and informed.

Start an issue

An "issue" is one opportunity, threat, or decision. It can be at the level of your city, province, or the whole country.
