What is luminocracy?

Luminocracy is a balanced, unbiased source of information about important issues in Canadian politics, and a tool to channel the collective political will into action.

We combine crowdsourced, fact-checked articles with open-letter writing, allowing you to express your informed opinions to political, economic, and cultural actors, while gaining the support of your peers. Luminocracy is a platform for driving credible campaigns for change.

Linking knowledge and action

It's frustrating to read the news and feel powerless. We're connecting factual information about important issues with tools to exert your political will.

Algorithmically unbiased

We're building the first source of truly unbiased political information. Skeptical? Great! It's powered by the skepticism of the crowd.

Crowd curated

Our content is sourced from the crowd, so it is inherently free from manipulation by interested powers.

Transparent

Our platform carries open, mature, and proportionate discussion. We invite participation from the government and corporations. We can handle the truth!
